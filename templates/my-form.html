Thunderstorm Predictor

This app predicts the percentage probability of occurrence of thunderstorm in the city Austin.
The user has to enter todays weather condition and click on submit button.
Temperatures have to be entered in fahrenheit.
Pressure in Inches
Wind speed in miles/hr
Precipitation in inches
Visibility in miles




















Since we can't comment on the intensity of thunderstorm with the limited data available,
so we have rather planned to display the probability of the same in form of color codes.
1.The color red signifies that the occurance of thunderstorm is very certian.
2.The color yellow signifies that there's a slight chance of having a thunderstorm the next day
3.The color green signifies that it is safe outside, and the occurance of a thundersorm is very unlikely.
